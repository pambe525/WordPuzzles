Enhancements
  • Changed interface when solving puzzle to show a full clue list instead of clue buttons
  • Solved clues are shown with a green check icon; revealed clues are shown with an eye icon
  • Also, solved and revealed clues are shown in lighter colored font
  • Revealed clues will show zero earned points
  • Click on a solved or revealed clue to see the answer and parsing (if available); click again to hide answers
  • Click on an unsolved clue to enter the answer
  • Incorrect answers are indicated by an error message and can be re-submitted
Minor Enhancements
  • Changed default width of screen to closely fit mobile phones
  • Clue pts for a clue are now suppressed to prevent detection of meta clues with higher pts
  • Total points for the word puzzle are still shown on the title so puzzler knows the goal
  • Progress bar now shows number of clues solved/revealed instead of points solved/revealed
  • More detail shown for puzzle scores for each puzzler on scores page

v1.0: [5/8/2022]

Recent Activity in Dashboard
  • Shows newly published puzzles within the last 7 days
  • Shows puzzles with unfinished solve sessions by current user
  • Scores can be viewed for published puzzles with active sessions
  • A green flag on a puzzle indicates a completed puzzle
  • A red glag on a puzzle session indicates an incompletely solved puzzle
  • A number in a blue circle on a puzzle indicates the number of puzzlers solving the puzzle
Puzzle Scores
  • Scores are listed in descending order (highest to lowest)
  • The list of scores includes all puzzlers solving the puzzles
  • A progress bar for each puzzler shows solved, revealed and remaining clues
  • Scores also include the total time spent by each puzzler on solving the puzzle
Solve a Puzzle
  • Puzzlers can solve a published puzzle (not edited by them)
  • Solved clues are shown as green clue badges
  • Revealed answers are shown as gray clue badges
  • Scores are automatically incremented when correct answer is submitted
  • Progress bar indicates the solved pts, revealed pts, and remaining pts
  • An active timer clocks the time spent in solving the puzzle
  • A FINISH LATER button allows pausing the timer

v0.8: [3/15/2022]

All Puzzles List
  • Shows all published puzzles in groups of 10 per page
  • Puzzles list can be sorted by various puzzle properties, in ascending or descending order.
  • Puzzlers can click on the puzzle title to solve or preview the puzzle.
  • This list can be accessed by the SHOW ALL PUZZLES button on Dashboard.

v0.7: [3/12/2022]

Dashboard
  • Dashboard is the home page and shows Recently Published puzzles and user's Draft Puzzles.
  • Puzzlers can see puzzles published within the last 7 days in the Recently Published section.
  • Puzzle editor can preview and unpublish a puzzle by clicking on the puzzle title.
  • Puzzlers can see their unpublished draft puzzles in the Draft Puzzles section, last edited first.
  • Puzzle editors can edit their puzzle by clicking on the title of the puzzle in the draft section.
  • Each puzzle in the draft list offers icon buttons to preview or delete the puzzle.

v0.5: [2/28/2022]

Word Puzzle Management
  • Puzzler can create a New Puzzle (crytic or non-cryptic clues).
  • Word puzzles can be edited and saved.
  • Puzzle editor can Add Clue(s) to the puzzle.
  • Points and parsing note can also be added to each clue.
  • Puzzle editor can delete the puzzle (after confirmation).
  • Puzzle editor can Preview and Publish the puzzle (after clues are created).
  • Once published, a puzzle is available to all logged in puzzlers to solve. Published puzzles cannot be edited.
  • Unpublish a puzzle to withdraw it from availibility to other puzzlers or to edit errors.
